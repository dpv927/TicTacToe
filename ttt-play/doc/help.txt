Usage: ttt <option> ... <option>
Options:
  --ai-mode                Play 1v1 with an AI.
  --player-mode            Play 1v1 with physical players.
  --depth=<depth>          Limits the search depth to a specific
                           number (Min 0, max 9)    
  --version                Display ttt version information.
